# 1. Information We Collect

We collect various types of personal information to provide and improve our services.
## 1.1 Personal Information You Provide

    Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and password.
    Recipes & Content: When you submit or save recipes, we collect that content, including any images or text you add.
    Communications: If you contact us directly, we may collect additional information, such as the content of your messages and your contact details.

## 1.2 Automatically Collected Information

    Usage Data: We collect information on how you use the platform, such as pages visited, time spent, and interactions.
    Device Information: We gather data from the device and browser you use to access our services, including your IP address, device type, operating system, and browser type.
    Cookies & Similar Technologies: We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience, store preferences, and analyze site usage.

# 2. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information for various purposes, including:

    To Provide and Improve Our Services: Ensuring functionality, personalizing user experiences, and improving performance.
    To Facilitate Sharing & Collaboration: Allowing users to share recipes, collaborate on collections, and interact with each other.
    To Send Notifications: Keeping you updated on shared recipes, collection updates, or other platform activities.
    For Marketing and Promotional Purposes: We may use your information to provide you with relevant offers or updates, but only if you’ve opted into these communications.
    To Analyze and Improve the Platform: Using analytics to understand how users engage with the platform, identifying trends, and improving overall functionality.
    For Security: Ensuring the safety and security of your account and our platform from unauthorized access or malicious activities.

# 3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your data in the following circumstances:

    With Service Providers: We may share your information with trusted service providers who help us operate our platform (e.g., hosting, analytics, payment processing).
    Legal Compliance: We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to a valid request from a governmental authority.
    Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

# 4. Your Rights & Choices

You have the following rights concerning your personal information:

    Access: You can access and review the information we hold about you at any time by logging into your account.
    Correction: If your information is inaccurate or incomplete, you have the right to request its correction.
    Deletion: You may request the deletion of your account and personal information at any time, though certain legal or operational reasons may require us to retain some data.
    Opt-Out: You can opt out of marketing communications by following the unsubscribe instructions in any emails we send or by adjusting your account settings.

# 5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy. Once data is no longer needed, we will securely delete or anonymize it.
# 6. Security

We implement a variety of security measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. Therefore, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.
# 7. Children’s Privacy

Our platform is not intended for use by children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we learn we have collected such data, we will take steps to delete it as soon as possible.
